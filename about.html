Our Story

From a reluctant car ride to a lifetime of adventures — this is our journey together.

How We Met

Dimitris and Aphrodite's love story began in the least romantic way possible: a 30-minute car ride neither of them wanted to be on. Dimitris had agreed to give Aphrodite a ride, and meanwhile, they were both separately texting their friends to complain about being stuck in a car together. What they didn't expect was that by the time the ride ended, they'd gone from reluctant passengers to practically inseparable — and the rest, as they say, is history.

They officially started dating in October 2020, right in the middle of COVID lockdowns, which meant their relationship fast-tracked itself. Even though they didn't technically move in together until later, it felt like they'd been living together from day one. Somewhere between surviving lockdowns, building a life side by side, and realizing they never got tired of each other, it became clear this was forever.

Dimitris proposed during a trip to an island, getting down on one knee on a cliff at sunset — after being noticeably nervous for days leading up to the moment. Aphrodite said yes, of course. Since then, life has only gotten sweeter, with an engagement, a growing family, and now counting down to their wedding in September 2026.
